## What is "Community Rewards"?

### Business Model (Compliant Explanation)

**MyGrowNet operates empowerment projects:**
- Agricultural ventures
- Manufacturing businesses
- Service companies
- Community enterprises

**When these projects succeed:**
- Company shares success with active members
- Distributed quarterly as "Community Rewards"
- Reward for being an active, engaged member
- NOT investment returns (members don't invest in projects)

**Key Distinction:**
- Members subscribe to MyGrowNet platform
- Company invests in projects (not members)
- Company shares success as membership benefit
- This is a reward program, not investment returns
