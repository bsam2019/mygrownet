# MyGrowNet Module Business Strategy

**Last Updated:** December 1, 2025
**Status:** Strategic Planning

## Executive Summary

MyGrowNet will offer subscription-based apps for personal growth and SME business management. Each app functions as both an integrated platform module and a standalone PWA, providing flexibility while maintaining a unified backend.

## Market Positioning

### Target Markets

**1. Personal Growth Segment**
- Young professionals (25-40 years)
- Entrepreneurs and side-hustlers
- Students and recent graduates
- Income: K2,000 - K15,000/month
- Tech-savvy, mobile-first users

**2. SME Business Segment**
- Small businesses (1-20 employees)
- Micro-enterprises and startups
- Retail shops, service providers
- Revenue: K50,000 - K500,000/month
- Need affordable business tools

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Risk:** Service downtime
- **Mitigation:** 99.9% uptime SLA, redundant servers, regular backups

- **Risk:** Data loss
- **Mitigation:** Daily backups, data replication, disaster recovery plan

- **Risk:** Security breaches
- **Mitigation:** Encryption, regular audits, penetration testing

### Business Risks
- **Risk:** Low adoption
- **Mitigation:** Free trials, competitive pricing, strong marketing

- **Risk:** High churn
- **Mitigation:** Excellent support, continuous improvement, engagement programs

- **Risk:** Competition
- **Mitigation:** Unique features, local focus, community integration

### Financial Risks
- **Risk:** Payment defaults
- **Mitigation:** Prepaid model, automatic renewals, grace periods

- **Risk:** Currency fluctuations
- **Mitigation:** Local currency pricing, hedging strategies
