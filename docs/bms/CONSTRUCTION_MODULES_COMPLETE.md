
Navigate to:
- `/cms/projects` - Project management
- `/cms/subcontractors` - Subcontractor database
- `/cms/equipment` - Equipment tracking
- `/cms/labour/crews` - Crew management
- `/cms/labour/timesheets` - Timesheet tracking

2. Click "New Project"
3. Fill in project details
4. Add milestones (optional)
