## Competitive Analysis

### Current Options in Zambia

| Platform | Pros | Cons | Cost |
|----------|------|------|------|
| **WordPress** | Powerful, flexible | Complex, requires maintenance | K500-2000/month |
| **Wix/Squarespace** | Easy to use | No local payments, expensive | $15-30/month |
| **Facebook Page** | Free, familiar | Limited, not professional | Free |
| **WhatsApp Business** | Free, direct | No website, limited features | Free |

### GrowBuilder Positioning

**Better than WordPress:** Simpler, local payments, no maintenance  
**Better than Wix:** Local focus, cheaper, MoMo integration  
**Better than Facebook:** Professional, own domain, more features  
**Better than WhatsApp:** Full website, catalog, booking system

## Risk Analysis

### Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Payment integration complexity** | High | Start with MTN MoMo only, add Airtel later |
| **Competition from WordPress** | Medium | Focus on local templates + ease of use |
| **Development delays** | High | Phased approach + MVP-first |
| **Users needing help** | Medium | Tutorials + ambassador program |
| **Low bandwidth issues** | High | Optimize for speed, test on 2G |
| **Payment gateway downtime** | Medium | Fallback to WhatsApp orders |
