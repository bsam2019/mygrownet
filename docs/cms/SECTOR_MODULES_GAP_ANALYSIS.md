## 🔴 **Critical Gaps - High Priority**

### **1. Production/Workshop Management** ⚠️
**Status:** Missing  
**Importance:** CRITICAL for aluminium fabrication

**What's Needed:**
- Production orders/work orders
- Workshop scheduling
- Cutting lists generation
- Material cutting optimization
- Production tracking (in progress, completed)
- Quality control checkpoints
- Waste tracking per job
- Production capacity planning

**Use Case:**
- Job comes in → Create production order
- Generate cutting list for profiles
- Optimize cuts to minimize waste
- Track production progress
- Quality check before delivery

**Impact:** Without this, fabricators can't manage their workshop efficiently.

---

### **2. Installation/Site Management** ⚠️
**Status:** Partial (Site Diary exists, but limited)  
**Importance:** CRITICAL for both sectors

**What's Needed:**
- Installation scheduling
- Site visit tracking
- Installation checklists
- Before/after photos
- Customer sign-off
- Defect/snag list management
- Warranty tracking
- Service call management

**Use Case:**
- Schedule installation team
- Track site visits
- Document installation with photos
- Get customer signature
- Track warranty period
- Handle service calls

**Impact:** Poor installation tracking leads to disputes and warranty issues.

### **3. Inventory Management** ⚠️
**Status:** Basic inventory exists, needs enhancement  
**Importance:** HIGH

**What's Needed:**
- Real-time stock levels
- Stock movements (in/out)
- Stock adjustments
- Stock valuation (FIFO/LIFO)
- Low stock alerts
- Stock transfer between locations
- Physical stock count
- Stock aging reports

**Use Case:**
- Receive materials → Update stock
- Issue materials to job → Reduce stock
- Get alerts when stock is low
- Transfer stock between warehouses
- Monthly stock reconciliation

**Impact:** Without proper inventory, you over-order or run out of materials.
