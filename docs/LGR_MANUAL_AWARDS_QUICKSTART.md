# LGR Manual Awards - Quick Start Guide

## For Administrators

### Accessing the Feature

1. Log in as admin
2. Navigate to **Admin Dashboard**
3. Click **LGR Management** in the sidebar
4. Select **Manual Awards**

### Awarding a Bonus

**Step 1: Click "Award Bonus"**

**Step 2: Select Member**
- Choose from dropdown of premium members
- View their current referral count and LGC balance

**Step 3: Enter Details**
- **Amount**: K10 - K2,100 (recommended: K500 for early adopters)
- **Type**: Choose appropriate category
  - Early Adopter - Launch phase members
  - Performance - Exceptional engagement
  - Marketing - Campaign incentives
  - Special - Unique recognition
- **Reason**: Explain why (required, 10-500 characters)

**Step 4: Review & Confirm**
- Check preview showing new balance
- Click "Award Bonus"
- Confirm the action

### What Happens

✅ Member's wallet is credited immediately  
✅ Transaction is recorded  
✅ Member receives notification  
✅ Full audit trail is maintained  

### Best Practices

**Recommended Amounts:**
- Early Adopter: K500 (one-time)
- Referral Milestone: K250
- Performance: K300-K500
- Marketing: K200-K400

**Documentation:**
- Always provide clear reason
- Be consistent across members
- Track monthly totals
- Review award history regularly

### Viewing Award History

The awards dashboard shows:
- Total awarded (lifetime)
- Total recipients
- This month's total
- Complete award history with filters

### Important Notes

⚠️ Only premium members are eligible  
⚠️ Awards are immediate and cannot be reversed  
⚠️ All actions are logged with your admin ID  
⚠️ Members can use LGC on platform or withdraw up to 40%  

## Use Cases

### Launch Phase (First 3 Months)
Award K500 to first 50 premium members who:
- Purchase premium starter kit
- Complete profile setup
- Refer at least 1 person
- Engage with platform features

### Referral Milestones
Award K250 when member reaches:
- 3 direct referrals
- 5 direct referrals
- 10 direct referrals

### Monthly Recognition
Award K300-K500 to top 5 members based on:
- Most active on platform
- Highest engagement score
- Community leadership
- Content contribution

### Marketing Campaigns
Award K200-K400 for:
- Social media promotion
- Event attendance
- Workshop participation
- Testimonial submission

## Support

For questions or issues:
- Check award history in admin panel
- Review member's transaction log
- Verify wallet balance
- Contact development team if needed
