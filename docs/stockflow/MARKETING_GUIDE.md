# StockFlow — Marketing & Sales Guide

## What is StockFlow?

StockFlow is a complete business inventory and cash management system built for small to medium businesses in Zimbabwe and across Africa. It runs on the MyGrowNet platform and provides everything a business needs to track stock, process sales, manage cash, and generate reports — all from a mobile phone or computer.

## Key Selling Points

### 1. All-in-One System
StockFlow replaces multiple tools: spreadsheets, receipt books, cash books, and manual stock cards. One login gives the business owner full visibility.

### 2. Works on Any Device
Access via any web browser — no app installation needed. Works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

### 3. No Hardware Cost
No need for expensive POS terminals, barcode scanners, or dedicated servers. Just a phone or computer with internet access.

### 4. Real-Time Inventory Tracking
Every sale, purchase, and adjustment updates stock levels instantly. Low stock alerts help prevent stockouts.

### 5. Automatic Cash Register
Track every cash movement: sales, expenses, banking. Know exactly what should be in the till at closing time.

### 6. Built-In Reports
Generate PDF reports for sales, purchases, inventory, and cash summaries. Perfect for tax filing and business reviews.

### 7. Audit-Ready
Full physical count and audit system with reconciliation reports. Know your actual stock vs what the system says.

### 8. Quotations, Invoices, Receipts
Create professional quotes, send invoices, and automatically generate receipts. Track everything from quote to payment.

### 9. Industry-Specific Extensions
StockFlow grows with your business. Add vertical-specific features through extensions:
- **Pharmacy** — controlled medicine tracking with dosage and schedule records
- **Manufacturing** — bill of materials, work orders, production costing
- **Restaurant** — recipe management, menu costing, wastage tracking
- More extensions in development (SuperMarket, Hardware, Electronics)

### 10. CSV Bulk Import
Download templates and import your inventory, suppliers, and bins via CSV. No manual data entry for large catalogs.

### 11. Free Trials
Each extension comes with a free trial period (default: 14 days). Companies can test features before subscribing.

### 12. Dual-Currency Pricing
Pricing available in both USD (international) and ZMW (Zambia) — pay in your local currency.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need internet all the time?**
A: Yes, an active internet connection is required. StockFlow works well on mobile data.

**Q: Can multiple employees use it at the same time?**
A: Yes. Each employee gets their own login with role-based permissions.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. Data is stored securely in the cloud with regular backups.

**Q: Can I print receipts?**
A: Yes. Receipts and reports can be printed or exported as PDF.

**Q: What if I need a custom feature?**
A: StockFlow is built on a modular architecture. Custom features can be developed.
