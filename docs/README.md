## üîë Key Concepts

### The Platform
- **What**: Community empowerment platform combining education, mentorship, and income opportunities
- **Structure**: Private limited company (NOT an investment scheme)
- **Model**: Subscription-based with product sales and profit-sharing

### The Network
- **Matrix**: 3√ó3 forced matrix, 7 levels deep
- **Capacity**: 3,279 total positions per member
- **Spillover**: Automatic placement of excess referrals

### The Progression
- **Levels**: 7 professional levels (Associate to Ambassador)
- **Advancement**: Based on Lifetime Points (LP) + time + requirements
- **Benefits**: Higher commissions and profit-sharing multipliers

### The Points System
- **LP (Lifetime Points)**: For level advancement, never expire
- **MAP (Monthly Activity Points)**: For earnings qualification, reset monthly
- **Earning**: Network building, product sales, education, engagement
- **Multipliers**: Streak bonuses (up to 1.5x) and performance tiers (up to +30%)

### The Income
- **6 Streams**: Referrals, commissions, milestones, bonuses, profit-sharing, product sales
- **Qualification**: Must earn minimum MAP each month to receive earnings
- **Profit-Sharing**: 60% of project profits distributed quarterly to active members
