## üîë Key Concepts

### The Platform
- **What**: Community empowerment platform combining education, mentorship, and income opportunities
- **Structure**: Private limited company (NOT an investment scheme)
- **Model**: Subscription-based with product sales and profit-sharing

### The Network
- **Matrix**: 3√ó3 forced matrix, 7 levels deep
- **Capacity**: 3,279 total positions per member
- **Spillover**: Automatic placement of excess referrals

### The Progression
- **Levels**: 7 professional levels (Associate to Ambassador)
- **Advancement**: Based on Lifetime Points (LP) + time + requirements
- **Benefits**: Higher commissions and profit-sharing multipliers

### The Points System
- **LP (Life Points)**: For level advancement, never expire, accumulate forever
- **BP (Bonus Points)**: For monthly earnings calculation, reset monthly
- **Earning**: Network building, product sales, education, engagement, subscriptions
- **Multipliers**: Subscription tier multipliers (1.0x to 2.0x)

### The Income
- **Multiple Streams**: 
  - Monthly BP bonuses (60% of profit pool)
  - Quarterly profit-sharing (LP-based)
  - Direct commissions
  - Product sales
  - Service income
  - Milestone rewards
- **Qualification**: Must earn minimum 50 BP to receive monthly bonus
- **Distribution**: Fair, transparent, performance-based

## üìä System Integration

```
Member Journey:
1. Register ‚Üí Associate Level (100 LP welcome bonus)
2. Earn Points ‚Üí Through activities (network, sales, learning, engagement)
   - LP accumulates forever (for level advancement)
   - BP accumulates monthly (for bonus calculation)
3. Advance Levels ‚Üí Meet LP + time + requirements
4. Qualify Monthly ‚Üí Earn minimum 50 BP to receive bonus
5. Receive Earnings ‚Üí Monthly BP bonus + quarterly profit-sharing + commissions
6. Grow Network ‚Üí 3√ó3 matrix with spillover
7. Repeat & Scale ‚Üí Higher levels + subscription tiers = better multipliers
```
