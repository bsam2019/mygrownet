## 8. Key Formulas

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    ESSENTIAL CALCULATIONS                        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Monthly Bonus:
    Member Bonus = (Individual BP / Total BP) × Bonus Pool
    
    Where:
    • Bonus Pool = Monthly Profit × 60%
    • Individual BP = Your BP earned this month
    • Total BP = Sum of all members' BP

Bonus Pool:
    Bonus Pool = (Revenue - Costs) × 60%
    Company Retention = (Revenue - Costs) × 40%

BP from Purchase:
    BP Earned = (Purchase Amount / 100) × 10
    
    Example: K500 purchase = (500/100) × 10 = 50 BP

Subscription Tier Multiplier:
    Final Points = Base Points × Tier Multiplier
    
    Example: 100 BP × 1.5x (Gold tier) = 150 BP

Minimum Qualification:
    IF BP >= 50 THEN eligible for bonus
    ELSE no bonus this month
```
