# MyGrowNet Organizational Structure

**Last Updated:** November 2, 2025  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Strategic Planning Document

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the recommended organizational structure for MyGrowNet across three growth phases: Short Term (0-6 months), Medium Term (6-18 months), and Long Term (18+ months). The structure is designed to support the platform's evolution from startup to enterprise scale while maintaining operational efficiency and compliance.

---

## Current Platform Features

### Core Systems
- 7-level professional progression (Associate → Ambassador)
- 3×3 forced matrix with spillover
- Dual-point system (Lifetime Points & Monthly Activity Points)
- Subscription-based membership model

### Financial Operations
- Digital wallet system (MyGrow Save)
- Commission processing (7-level referral system)
- Quarterly profit-sharing
- Payment approvals and withdrawals
- Mobile money integration

### Products & Services
- Starter kits with tiered access
- Learning resources and workshops
- E-commerce platform
- Digital products and certifications

### Investment Features
- Business Growth Fund (BGF)
- Venture Builder (co-investment platform)
- Community investment projects
- Portfolio management

### Administrative Tools
- Comprehensive admin dashboards
- KYC/verification system
- Fraud detection
- Compliance monitoring
- Analytics and reporting

## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

#### 1. Regulatory Risk
**Risk:** Changes in financial regulations affecting operations

**Mitigation:**
- Maintain strong compliance function
- Regular engagement with regulators
- Legal counsel on retainer
- Proactive compliance monitoring
- Industry association membership

---

#### 2. Operational Risk
**Risk:** System failures, fraud, operational errors

**Mitigation:**
- Robust internal controls
- Segregation of duties
- Regular audits
- Fraud detection systems
- Business continuity planning
- Insurance coverage

---

#### 3. Financial Risk
**Risk:** Cash flow issues, commission sustainability

**Mitigation:**
- Conservative financial planning
- Cash reserve (6+ months runway)
- Diversified revenue streams
- Regular financial monitoring
- Scenario planning

---

#### 4. Technology Risk
**Risk:** Security breaches, system downtime

**Mitigation:**
- Regular security audits
- Penetration testing
- Disaster recovery plan
- Redundant infrastructure
- 24/7 monitoring
- Incident response plan

---

#### 5. Reputational Risk
**Risk:** Negative publicity, member complaints

**Mitigation:**
- Excellent customer service
- Transparent communication
- Crisis management plan
- Social media monitoring
- PR strategy
- Member education

---

#### 6. Market Risk
**Risk:** Competition, market saturation

**Mitigation:**
- Continuous innovation
- Strong value proposition
- Member loyalty programs
- Strategic partnerships
- Market research
- Diversification
