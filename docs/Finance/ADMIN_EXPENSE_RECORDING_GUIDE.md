# Admin Guide: Recording Platform Expenses

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-01  
**Audience:** MyGrowNet Administrators

## Overview

MyGrowNet uses the CMS (Company Management System) to track all platform operating expenses. This includes marketing costs, office supplies, infrastructure, legal fees, and more.

All approved expenses automatically appear in the Profit & Loss dashboard.

## How to Record an Expense

### Step 1: Access CMS

From the Admin Dashboard:
1. Click **Finance** in the sidebar
2. Click **Record Expense** (has a CMS badge)

You'll be automatically logged into CMS as the MyGrowNet Platform company.

### Step 2: Fill in Expense Details

Required fields:
- **Category:** Select from dropdown (Marketing, Office, Travel, etc.)
- **Amount:** Enter expense amount in Kwacha
- **Description:** Brief description of the expense
- **Date:** When the expense occurred
- **Payment Method:** How it was paid (Cash, Bank Transfer, MTN MoMo, etc.)

Optional fields:
- **Receipt Number:** Reference number from receipt
- **Receipt:** Upload receipt image/PDF (max 5MB)
- **Notes:** Additional details
- **Job:** Link to specific job/project (if applicable)

### Step 3: Submit for Approval

Click **Submit Expense**

The expense will be marked as "Pending" and require approval.

### Step 4: Approve Expense

If you have approval rights:
1. Go to **Finance → Manage Expenses**
2. Find the pending expense
3. Click **Approve**

**What happens automatically:**
- ✅ Expense syncs to financial system
- ✅ Appears in Profit & Loss reports
- ✅ Included in expense analytics

## Expense Categories

Choose the appropriate category:

| Category | Use For |
|----------|---------|
| **Marketing** | Ads, promotions, campaigns, social media |
| **Office Supplies** | Stationery, equipment, furniture |
| **Travel** | Transport, accommodation, meals |
| **Infrastructure** | Hosting, servers, software licenses |
| **Legal** | Legal fees, compliance costs |
| **Professional Fees** | Consultants, contractors, services |
| **Utilities** | Internet, electricity, water |
| **Other** | Miscellaneous expenses |

## Viewing Expenses in P&L

1. Navigate to **Finance → Profit & Loss**
2. Select time period (Today, Week, Month, Quarter, Year)
3. Scroll to **Expenses** section
4. Find **Platform Expenses (CMS)**
5. Click **View Details →** to see breakdown in CMS

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do:
- Record expenses promptly (same day if possible)
- Upload receipts for all expenses
- Use clear, descriptive descriptions
- Choose the correct category
- Approve expenses within 24 hours

### ❌ Don't:
- Record personal expenses
- Duplicate expenses
- Use vague descriptions like "Stuff" or "Things"
- Forget to upload receipts
- Delay approvals

## Approval Workflow

### Who Can Approve?
- Platform Owners
- Finance Managers
- Designated Approvers

### Approval Limits
Some categories may have automatic approval for small amounts:
- Under K100: Auto-approved
- K100-K500: Manager approval
- Over K500: Owner approval

(Check with your finance team for current limits)

### Approval Notifications
Approvers receive notifications when:
- New expense submitted
- Expense requires their approval
- Expense approaching approval deadline

## Troubleshooting

### "Expense not appearing in P&L"

**Possible causes:**
1. Expense not approved yet (only approved expenses sync)
2. Sync delay (wait 1-2 minutes)
3. Wrong time period selected in P&L

**Solution:**
- Check expense status in CMS
- Refresh P&L dashboard
- Contact tech support if issue persists

### "Can't access CMS"

**Possible causes:**
1. Not logged in as admin
2. Session expired
3. Browser cache issue

**Solution:**
- Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser

### "Receipt upload failed"

**Possible causes:**
1. File too large (max 5MB)
2. Wrong file type (only JPG, PNG, PDF)
3. Network issue

**Solution:**
- Compress image/PDF
- Convert to supported format
- Check internet connection
